How to use

  1. Select text on any webpage.
  2. A small Study button appears — click it, pick a mode.
  3. Or press ⌘/Ctrl+Shift+S for a quick summary.
  4. Or right-click your selection → Lumen Study.
  5. Bookmark any result to save it to your library.

Your privacy

All saved summaries live in your browser's local storage. API keys are stored in Chrome's synced settings (encrypted by Chrome). Your selected text is sent only to the provider you chose, and only when you click a mode.
